My FAMILY

Love my family very much and can do anything for my family

The Son family is introduced in the series with the character Son Goku, whose name is the Japanese on'yomi rendering of Sun Wukong, a character from popular Chinese and Japanese cultures. Unlike most associated characters, the Son family does not have a theme of naming.
The Sons are noted as a family from Mount Paozu in the series. Supporting the fact that the Sons are an Eastern family, the Sons' last name is placed in front of the individual's name, which is consistent with family names in the real-life Eastern cultures. Furthermore, the Sons tend to wear traditional Asian styled garbs for their regular clothing.

THREE SAIYANS

Super Saiyan

First Appeared: Dragon Ball Z Episode 95 ("Transformed At Last")

Goku's initial transformation into a Super Saiyan remains a high point of Dragon Ball as a whole. Frieza pushed Goku a little too far by killing Krillin, which triggered this legendary transformation. At a time when transformations weren't a regular occurrence, the weight of this moment laid the foundation for everything to come in Dragon Ball. To make matters more interesting, the Super Saiyan form was also utilized throughout the entire Android Saga.

Super Saiyan Blue

First Appeared: Dragon Ball Z: Resurrection 'F'

Super Saiyan Blue, or Super Saiyan God Super Saiyan, takes things back to a simpler time. Less complicated than other high-level transformations, Super Saiyan Blue looks much like the Super Saiyan transformation, only with new blue hair. First used by Goku against Golden Frieza, it immediately became the flagship transformation of Dragon Ball Super.

Perfected Ultra Instinct

First Appeared: Dragon Ball Super Episode 129 ("A Transcendent Limit Break! Autonomous Ultra Instinct Mastered!")

When Goku first tapped into Ultra Instinct during the Tournament of Power, it was clear to many in attendance that Goku's Ultra Instinct was incomplete. Goku's Ultra Instinct -Sign- had numerous flaws, which led to it falling short of Jiren's immense powers. Toward the end of the Tournament of Power, Goku was able to channel into Ultra Instinct to fully complete it.
